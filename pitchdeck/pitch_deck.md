# ZCyptoBot — Investor-Grade Pitch Deck (Narrative)

## 1) Vision
Build the operating system for autonomous, institution-grade crypto execution with measurable alpha, strict risk control, and compliant automation.

## 2) Problem
- Fragmented liquidity and volatile microstructure create execution drag.
- Existing bots optimize for hobbyist UX, not institutional governance.
- Teams lack real-time observability and AI-driven remediation.

## 3) Solution
ZCyptoBot combines:
1. Multi-exchange execution layer
2. AIops orchestration with policy guardrails
3. Real-time risk + compliance controls
4. Unified observability (SLO, incident, cost, alpha attribution)

## 4) Product
- **Execution Core:** Smart routing, slippage minimization, venue health weighting.
- **Risk Engine:** Circuit breakers, drawdown governors, exposure limits.
- **AIOps Layer:** Automated anomaly detection and strategy adaptation.
- **Enterprise Controls:** Tenant policies, immutable audit trail, role-based access.

## 5) Market
- Primary: crypto-native funds, prop desks, market makers.
- Adjacent: exchanges, broker-dealers, fintech treasury operations.
- Expansion: regulated digital asset infrastructure.

## 6) Business Model
- SaaS platform fee (tiered by AUM + feature set)
- Usage pricing (events/sec, strategies, data retention)
- Performance-linked premium tier

## 7) Traction Milestones (Target)
- 10 pilot customers in 6 months
- 3 institutional logos in 12 months
- >99.95% platform availability and <100ms median route decision time

## 8) Go-To-Market
- Beachhead: elite crypto funds via technical founder-led sales.
- Channel: infra partners + exchange referral programs.
- Expansion: compliance and risk modules driving upsell.

## 9) Defensibility

- Deep infra integrations across exchanges and data systems
