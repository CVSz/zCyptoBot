# EU Data Residency Policy

## Residency Requirement
- Personal data classified as PII must be processed, stored, and logged in EU regions only.
- Cross-border transfer is deny-by-default and requires documented legal mechanism.

## Enforcement
- Regional geo-routing for request admission.
- Policy engine checks on every data movement event.
- Region-scoped compute, storage, key management, and backups.

## Exceptions
- Emergency break-glass requires DPO + security approval and time-bound scope.
- All exceptions must be audited with post-incident review.
