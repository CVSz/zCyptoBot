# GDPR Baseline Controls

## Lawful Basis
- Contractual necessity for service delivery.
- Consent for optional processing and analytics.
- Legitimate interest only with balancing test and documented rationale.

## Core Principles
- Data minimization and purpose limitation by default.
- Storage limitation enforced with retention schedules.
- Accuracy controls and correction workflows.
- Integrity and confidentiality with encryption and least privilege.

## Technical & Organizational Measures
- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (KMS-backed keys).
- Role-based access control with just-in-time elevation.
- Immutable audit logs for processing and administrative actions.
- Records of Processing Activities (RoPA) maintained per product and region.

## Subject Rights
- Access/export, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, portability.
- SLA-backed request handling and evidence collection for closure.
